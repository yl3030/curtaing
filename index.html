Introducing a 100% self-serve platform to customise your curtains and blinds. This is how it works.

  • 1
    SELECT YOUR MATERIAL

    Browse the materials on our website and use the “ADD TO ORDER” button to save your favorites.

  • 2
    START CUSTOMISING (Free Samples)

    Transform your saved materials into products on the Order Page, inputting measurements and other specifications.

  • 3
    PAY AND RECEIVE

    Submit your payment and shipping details, and your purchase will be delivered to your door in 4-6 weeks.

Style

Sustainability, affordability, and functionality are at the core of what we do. To create stunning custom products, we offer the perfect amount of luxury—complete with vivid hues and textures.

Samples

Ordering samples is very simple, browse all the materials on our website and use the “ADD TO ORDER” button to save your favorites, then visit Order Page to finalise. Receive up to 5 free samples today!

Specialty

For orders over $699.

Innovation

Curtaing’s very own game-changing tape ruler is a complimentary bonus for orders that include tracks & rods. Curtain installation will never be this easy and precise.
